Places to visit in Spain

Peaks of Europe

The Picos de Europa, also The Picos are a mountain range extending about 20km, forming part of the Cantabrian Mountains in northern Spain.

Nature

The Nature in Cantabria has brown bears and wolves that lives in the more remote regions. Rebeccos are fairly frequently seen and buzzards are common with various eagles and vultures arround.

Gastronomy

The Picos support a dwindling group of shepherds who move up from the valleys in the summer with their sheep, goats, cows and occasional pig. The area is famed for its piquant blue cheeses suchs as Cabrales cheese.

Gaztelugatxe

If you've seen or not the Game of Thrones series we highly recommend you to visit Gaztelugatxe

Gaztelugatxe is an islet on the coast of Biscay belonging to the municipality of Bermeo. It's connected to the mainland by a man-made bridge. On top of the island stands a hermitage (named Gaztelugatxe Doniene) dedicated to John Baptist, that dates from the 10th centiry.

With another small neighboring island, Aketx, they form a protected biotope that extends from the town of Bakio until Cape Matxitxako.
In these times of COVID-19 if you want to visit Gaztelugatxe you have to book a ticket from the main web site.

You must be fit to be able to walk the thousands of stairs that await you!
